Abilities

Toad Punch:

Every toad’s trademark attack. A classic that allows you to attack enemies when your close to them. While you may think this is a common move, it has uses in a sticky situation!


Throat float:

A jump too long to reach? No problem! Maybe it calls for Todd to float across the air. Todd can store air in his mouth and fly. It doesn’t last long though so plan out your movement carefully!


Tongue tackle:

A toad’s tongue can perform feats of strength, and this is no exception! Grab, hold and swing an enemy or object in any direction. Knock other enemies over or perhaps even use it to solve puzzles!


The Gumshoe Slinger and Slimeball:

Use this to shoot puppets far away that are up to no good! This takes up ammo in the form of slimeballs. So do your best in stocking up for various lands. You’ll need them when puppets start fighting dirty!
